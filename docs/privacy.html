Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

PriceWizard (“the app”) is a macOS application that helps you manage App Store Connect subscription prices across territories. This policy describes how we handle information in relation to the app and this website.

Data we don’t collect

We do not collect, store, or receive any personal data or usage data from you. We don’t use analytics, tracking, or cookies for the purpose of identifying users. This website may be hosted by a provider that collects standard server logs (e.g. IP, request time); we don’t use those for anything beyond basic operation.

Data on your device

When you use the app, your App Store Connect API credentials (Key ID, Issuer ID, and .p8 private key) are stored only in your Mac’s Keychain. We never transmit or have access to these credentials. All data the app uses (app list, subscriptions, prices) is fetched from Apple’s API on your machine and is not sent to us.

Third-party services

The app communicates only with:

  • Apple’s App Store Connect API — to read and update your subscription and price data, using your credentials.
  • A public exchange-rate service — to convert prices to USD for preview. We do not send any personal or account data to this service.

This website may load fonts from Google Fonts; see Google’s privacy policy for their practices.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app or site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at bonney977@gmail.com.
